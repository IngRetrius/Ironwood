Bridging the Gap: Ironwood Infrastructure

Ironwood Infrastructure was founded in 2022 to bridge a critical gap in the railway industry—delivering safety, compliance, and accountability while fostering stronger collaboration among stakeholders. As railway infrastructure projects grow in scale and complexity, the need for a solutions-driven partner has never been greater.

We bring together railways, road authorities, industry leaders, and regulators to create safer, more efficient, and forward-thinking railway networks.

With unmatched expertise in regulated railway infrastructure, we have positioned ourselves as a trusted engineering firm committed to excellence. Our deep industry knowledge and proactive approach sets us apart, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of safety, compliance, and accountability.

At Ironwood Infrastructure, we're not just delivering railway projects—we're shaping the future of transportation, strengthening connections between communities, and redefining what's possible.

Meet Our Principal

Jennifer Benedict, M.Eng., P.Eng.

Principal

Jennifer Benedict is a professional engineer with 15+ years of experience. She has an extensive background in risk and project management, inspection and regulatory compliance.

Her experience includes delivering over 120 bridge and grade crossing projects, in addition to the implementation of Transport Canada's Grade Crossing Regulations and Standards for over 7500 crossings.

Jennifer has a Master of Civil Engineering from the University of Toronto.

With an emphasis on safety and regulatory compliance, Jennifer's unwavering integrity and commitment to accountability drives success at Ironwood Infrastructure.

Jennifer holds the certificate of authorization for the corporation.
